Toward a New Regional Order

THE SEVENTH ANNUAL SULAIMANI FORUM

The Sulaimani Forum is the signature annual event hosted by the Institute of Regional and International Studies (IRIS) at AUIS.

8 th - 10 th DEC 2022

Kurdistan, Sulaimani

Program Brochure

Global Figures

Famous local and global figures attend the forum every year. This brings prominent leaders, intellectuals and practitioners to the cultural hub of Sulaimani.

Dialogue Sessions

Participants engage in meaningful dialogue sessions to tackle the most complex issues facing the region. This year's Forum provided a venue to plot the course ahead on key issues in the region.

Breaking Political Walls

Participants will get a chance to connect and network throughout the two days event. This will be a great opportunity build relations between opposing political parts in Iraq.

Introducing Iraq's History

Participants get a chance to go back through time and get introduced to the rich culture Iraq and Kurdistan has had and how it can be taken advantage of to move forward.
